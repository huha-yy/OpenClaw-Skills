## Description: <br>
Remove AI writing patterns from text. Use when editing, reviewing, or rewriting text to sound more natural and human-written. Detects patterns like inflated symbolism, promotional language, em dash overuse, AI vocabulary, and sycophantic tone. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and developers use this skill to revise text so it sounds more natural while preserving meaning and intended tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewrites may add opinions, first-person phrasing, or claims that are inaccurate or inappropriate for the context. <br>
Mitigation: Review rewritten text before publishing or sending it, especially where authorship, factual claims, or AI-use disclosure matters. <br>
